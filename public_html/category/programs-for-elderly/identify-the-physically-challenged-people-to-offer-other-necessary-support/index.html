Program

Identify the Physically Challenged People to offer other necessary support

Back to programs

5 January 2024

We are dedicated to identifying physically challenged individuals and providing them with the assistance they require to improve their quality of life. Through our activities, which include giving daycare and Primary Health care support to people with disabilities, we strive to engage with people who need help, such as those with mobility issues, sensory impairments, chronic illnesses, or other physical obstacles. We aim to ensure that every person with a physical disability gets the help they need to overcome obstacles, achieve their goals, and thrive in all parts of life.\" In addition
