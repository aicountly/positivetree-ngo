About Bharath Raghavan

Founder of Supporting Old Age Homes & Orphanages

My name is Bharath Raghavan, and I am the founder of the Positive Tree Foundation. This entire journey of being a founder started with just one intention: supporting old age homes and orphanages to make them their superheroes. I feel that every person has the power to lead a life of success and can channel their energy into self-growth.

So with my 18 years of analysis, I learned that education, positivity, and moral support could help them succeed. So I dreamed of building a High vibe community around India to lead the way to NGO development. Rather than focusing on collecting funds and donating, we are building a positive community for all kids to survive on their own.

Whatever it takes for them to be independent (as much as possible), we are building the next generation of kids who can rule the world.
