About Positive Foundation

When Ambition Meets Opportunity Everything is To Be Possible In All

Positive Tree Foundation is a community that believes in providing a safe, comfortable, and nurturing environment for seniors and children who have experienced adverse life circumstances. We built invaluable, in-depth, and long-lasting connections with everyone that becomes a part of our community, as we believe that everyone deserves to feel valued and cared for, regardless of age or background.

Our team members are caring and experienced experts dedicated to satisfying our residents' physical, emotional, and spiritual needs.
