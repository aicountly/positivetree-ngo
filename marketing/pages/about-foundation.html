Foundation Overview

Empowering Futures, Building Foundations

Our Community

Positive Tree Foundation is a community that believes in providing a safe, comfortable, and nurturing environment for seniors and children who have experienced adverse life circumstances. We built invaluable, in-depth, and long-lasting connections with everyone that becomes a part of our community, as we believe that everyone deserves to feel valued and cared for, regardless of age or background.

Our Team

Our team members are caring and experienced experts dedicated to satisfying our residents' physical, emotional, and spiritual needs. We try tirelessly to create a homely atmosphere that encourages a sense of community and belonging. Our foundation provides invaluable services — Education, positivity, moral support, and care to people of all age groups regardless of gender, race, or ethnicity.

Our Mission

"Changing lives, one heart at a time: Our mission is to make an exponential difference in the lives of individuals, providing hope and resilience."

Our mission at Positive Tree Foundation is to positively change the lives of those we serve through personalized care, respect, and a deep understanding of their unique needs. We aim to ensure our residents can access the resources, services, and care they need to live enriching and fulfilling lives.

Our Vision

"Supporting People, transforming lives: Our vision is to create a space where every individual can thrive, regardless of their circumstances."

Our vision at Positive Tree Foundation is to be a leading provider of people-oriented care for seniors and children in need. We aim to expand our foundation to various cities across India to stretch our warmth and compassion to all the people in need.
